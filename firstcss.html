"Nature thought in English" refers to common English expressions and philosophical ideas about the natural world, often found in quotes and proverbs. These expressions emphasize nature's role as a teacher, a source of healing and peace, and a place of wildness and freedom, as seen in famous sayings like "Let nature be your teacher" (Wordsworth) or "Nature does not hurry, yet everything is accomplished"
